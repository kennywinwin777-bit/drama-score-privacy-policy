Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2025

Welcome to DramaScore. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy governs our privacy practices regarding our mobile application (the "App") and explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By accessing or using DramaScore, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the application.

01 Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us or that is generated automatically when you use DramaScore.

Personal Data Provided Directly

  • Account Profiles: Usernames, Email addresses, Profile Pictures, and Unique User IDs (UIDs) created via Firebase Auth or Google Sign-In.
  • Feedback & Support: Message content, email address, or device specifications provided when sending feedback or requesting assistance.
  • Gamification Predictions: Predictions submitted for sports matches (e.g., Daily Cup scorelines).

Automatically Collected Data

  • Log & Usage Data: Pages visited, features accessed, buttons clicked, and match streams/visual effects trigger actions.
  • Device Details: Device type, Operating System (iOS/Android), IP addresses, screen resolutions, and advertising identifiers (e.g., Google AD-ID or Apple IDFA).
  • Crash Reports: Performance files and error stack-traces automatically handled through Google Firebase Crashlytics.

03 In-App Economy & Purchases

DramaScore offers a virtual currency system ("My Points") which users can acquire by logging in daily, viewing reward ads, or purchasing via iOS and Android App Store billing integrations.

  • In-App Purchase Records: Purchasing points packs (Starter, Growth, Master Packs) uses the respective Google Play Store Billing or Apple App Store In-App Purchases. We do not process or store credit cards or payment credentials directly. All transaction authorizations are kept safe and handled by Apple or Google.
  • Points Store, Virtual Items & Visual Effects: Users can spend Points to unlock generative AI Scout Reports, obtain "Lucky Charms", or trigger interactive live-match visual elements like "Blessings" (via Blessing Sparks) or special visual effects. These visual assets are linked strictly to your unique account ID.

04 Generative AI Features

Our App uses high-performance generative artificial intelligence models provided by Google Gemini to produce interesting dynamic summaries. To produce commentary:

  • We send anonymized event logs (e.g., "The match has a possession swing of 70% for Team A") to the secure Google API.
  • Absolutely no personally identifiable information (PII), including account names or emails, is ever shared with the AI models.

05 Data Deletion & Retention

We retain your data (such as account profiles and transaction history) as long as your account is active. To comply with modern App Store policies and GDPR rules, you can request full account and data erasure at any time.

How to delete your account and associated data:

Please contact us directly via email at support@dramascore.com with your registered username and email. Once verified, we will permanently delete your authentication record, username profile, virtual currency balances, and transaction database records within 7 business days.

06 Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

07 Changes to This Policy
